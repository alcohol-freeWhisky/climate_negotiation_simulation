The Conference of the Parties serving as the meeting of the Parties to the Paris Agreement,

Recalling the provisions of Articles 6.8 and 6.9 of the Paris Agreement,

Recognizing the importance of integrated, holistic and balanced non-market approaches being available to Parties to assist in the implementation of their nationally determined contributions, in the context of sustainable development and poverty eradication,

Acknowledging that non-market approaches are an integral part of the cooperative approaches under Article 6 of the Paris Agreement,

1. Decides to establish a framework for non-market approaches referred to in Article 6.9 of the Paris Agreement, to promote the non-market approaches referred to in Article 6.8;

2. Decides that the framework for non-market approaches shall aim to:
(a) Promote non-market approaches in the context of sustainable development and poverty eradication;
(b) Enhance coordination among Parties, stakeholders and relevant institutional arrangements;
(c) Facilitate the identification, development and implementation of non-market approaches;

3. Requests the Subsidiary Body for Scientific and Technological Advice to develop the work programme for the framework for non-market approaches, for consideration and adoption by the Conference of the Parties serving as the meeting of the Parties to the Paris Agreement at its third session;

4. Invites Parties and admitted observer organizations to submit via the submission portal their views on the work programme, including on the following elements:
(a) The scope of non-market approaches;
(b) Governance and institutional arrangements;
(c) Linkages and complementarity with other mechanisms under Article 6;
(d) Reporting requirements;

5. Also requests the secretariat to organize workshops in conjunction with the sessions of the subsidiary bodies to facilitate the exchange of views on non-market approaches;

6. Decides that the non-market approaches framework shall complement and not duplicate existing arrangements under the Convention and the Paris Agreement;

7. Requests the Subsidiary Body for Scientific and Technological Advice to report on progress in implementing this decision to the Conference of the Parties serving as the meeting of the Parties to the Paris Agreement at its third session.
