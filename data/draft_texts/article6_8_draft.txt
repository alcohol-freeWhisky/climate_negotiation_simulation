The Conference of the Parties serving as the meeting of the Parties to the Paris Agreement,

Recalling the provisions of Articles 6.8 and 6.9 of the Paris Agreement,

Recognizing the importance of integrated, holistic and balanced non-market approaches being available to Parties to assist in the implementation of their nationally determined contributions, in the context of sustainable development and poverty eradication,

[Acknowledging that non-market approaches contribute to the implementation of the Paris Agreement and can complement market-based approaches,]

1. [Option 1: Decides to establish a framework for non-market approaches to sustainable development referred to in Article 6.9 of the Paris Agreement, to promote the non-market approaches referred to in Article 6.8;]
[Option 2: Decides to establish a platform for non-market approaches to facilitate the identification, development and implementation of non-market approaches;]

2. [Decides that the framework/platform for non-market approaches shall aim to:(a) Promote non-market approaches in the areas of [mitigation, adaptation, finance, technology transfer and capacity-building] [sustainable development and poverty eradication];
(b) [Enhance coordination among Parties and stakeholders] [Facilitate the sharing of experiences and best practices];
(c) [Option 1: Facilitate the implementation of non-market approaches that support the implementation of NDCs;]
[Option 2: Facilitate the identification, development and implementation of non-market approaches;]]

3. [Requests the [Subsidiary Body for Scientific and Technological Advice] [a dedicated body] to develop the work programme for the framework for non-market approaches, for consideration [and adoption] by the Conference of the Parties serving as the meeting of the Parties to the Paris Agreement;]

4. [Invites Parties [and observers] to submit [via the submission portal] their views on the work programme, including on the following elements:
(a) The scope of non-market approaches;
(b) [Governance and institutional arrangements;]
(c) [Linkages with other mechanisms under Article 6;]
(d) Reporting [and review] requirements;]

5. [Also requests the secretariat to organize [a workshop] [workshops and other activities] [in conjunction with the sessions of the subsidiary bodies] to facilitate the exchange of views on non-market approaches;]

6. [Decides that the non-market approaches framework shall [not duplicate] [complement and not substitute] existing arrangements under the Convention and the Paris Agreement;]

7. [Requests the Subsidiary Body for Scientific and Technological Advice to report on progress in implementing this decision to the Conference of the Parties serving as the meeting of the Parties to the Paris Agreement at its [next/third] session.]
